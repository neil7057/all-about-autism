Test Your Knowledge With The Quiz

There are 12 multiple choice questions selected at random from a bank of over 100 questions.

Accuracy is more important than speed but there is a time limit of 30 seconds for each question. The timer turns (red) when time is running out.

Select the answer you think is correct and it will be highlighted as correct or incorrect. Select the "next" button to progress to the next question.

You can only select one answer per question and once selected, you cannot change it.

If you run out of time for a question, then the correct answer is highlighted and you can move to the "next" question.

After you have answered all 12 questions, your total score will be calculated and displayed.

You can play again or exit the page via the main page menu. You'll be presented with a selection of random questions each time you play.
